Meet the Band

Phil Piltawer

Vocals

Meet Phil, our electrifying frontman and certified wild card. With his roaring vocals and boundless energy, he brings every show to life. From launching off the drum kit, to hilariously awkward mid-song jokes, he knows how to keep the crowd entertained! Funny, fearless, and a powerhouse on the mic, Phil makes sure no one forgets an Echo Chamber gig!

Afonso Meireles

Guitar

Meet Afonso, our shredder-in-chief. With ripping solos and thunderous riffs, Afonso's playing brings the sonic firepower that drives Echo Chamber's edge. Whether he's locked in the rhythm or unleashing face-melting leads, his playing never fails to turn heads and leave the crowd wanting more.

Ben Harn

Bass, Vocals

Meet Ben, the dual threat powerhouse! With earth-shaking basslines, and growling vocals, Ben brings the grit that powers the bands heavier edge, and locks down the low end. He doesn't just look like Stone Cold - he is the Royal Rumble!

Derek Sanson

Drums

Meet Derek, the drum-pounding engine of Echo Chamber. As our powerhouse drummer and designated hype man, he keeps the band tight, the tempo driving, and the energy sky-high. Whether he's locking down the pocket, or delivering flashy fills, Derek brings the fire behind the kit.
